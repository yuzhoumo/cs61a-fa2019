CS 70 at UC Berkeley

Resources

In this section of the website, you will find a problem bank, a collection of exams from previous semesters of CS70, as well as some guidelines to help you to use LaTeX for submitting your homeworks (highly recommended). You are strongly encouraged to use the past exams as preparation for this semester’s exams; however, you should be mindful of the fact that, while the CS70 syllabus has been quite stable for many years, the detailed content does vary slightly and you may find some topics in the exams that were not covered this semester: you may safely ignore these. Similarly, the precise coverage of various midterms may vary from semester to semester. Ultimately it is this semester’s lectures, notes, sections and homework that determine what you are expected to know for this semester’s course.
